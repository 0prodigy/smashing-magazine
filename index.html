PERSON OF THE WEEK

Inayaili de León (@yaili) is a Design Producer at Github. Previously, she was a Senior Designer at Microsoft Developer Services where she worked on the design system for Azure DevOps. At Canonical, she led the team creating the Vanilla design system. Inayaili shares her knowledge speaking at conferences and writing for well-known online publications and on her own blog. She also wrote the books Moving to Responsive Web Design and Pro CSS for High Traffic Websites. Thank you for everything you do for the web community, dear Inayaili!
